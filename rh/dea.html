Project Idea:

DEA is an online platform that allows the parents/grandparents or any caregivers to customize books for their kids based on kids character, age, gender, ethnicity and even dream jobs. It allows them to send special messages to the kids through the books. Unlike others, the book is not delivered in the form of a printed picturebook but instead in the form of a movie roll accompanied with a Do-It-Yourself Projector kit, that the parent builds with the kid on their own and they can then use their mobile flashes to project their ideas and imagination onto the world.. Fostering education and creativity, We create an interactive, intimate customizable storytelling experience that transports kids into a world of imagination. Our stories will have “you”, literally you, embarking on a culturally immersive adventure every time. It is a build-and-play tool with a kid-centric design that has a lot of potential for innovation and scalability.

We developed our own plots, our own automated customization algorithm, our illustrations - by many talented illustrators, but most of all our own projector design.

All the materials for the product development were locally resourced and sustainable from small producers.

You can finds samples of the plots in the creative section.

We are targetting a B2B product distribution schema in the beginning before going to expand to b2C market. We also have a lot of innovative ideas on how we can scale this to include different product versions, selling sound effect props with the product or even gesture recognition as we did here. I am also very thankful for the illustrators: Maha Hashem and Rawan Slama as well as the product designer Salma Saad for their diligent work.
