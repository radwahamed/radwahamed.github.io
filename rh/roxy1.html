“DAH DAH DAH DAH DAH DAH DAH DAH CIRCUS” the tune would loudly play. All over the quintillion galaxies, this is the most enjoyable place to be; that is what the children say. From the planets of Polka Dots, here comes the manager Mr. Hawks. Members of the circus form one happy family from different planets all over the galaxy.

The Boxy’s family made the dresses, the decorations and the tools. No show would go on without Mrs. Boxy’s jewels. The beautiful little Roxy lived with her mother and father, Mr. & Mrs. Boxy. “They are very sweet” all the circus members would claim; “just like anyone who, from the cubic planet, came”. In their little cubic red tent, the magic of the circus never came to an end.

Beautiful Little Roxy, on the other hand, came back home one day really sad. She wiped a salty tear and told her mum with fear: “Why can all the kids perform on a show while all I can do is sprinkle the glitter on the dresses to glow?” Her mother hugged her and breathed some air saying: “wipe the frown off this little square. Be proud Roxy; all the cubes do the same. Even our family is in the Hall of Fame.”

As Mrs. Boxy was putting her in bed, she found her bedtime story lying there unread. “Sleep now Roxy; tomorrow is a busy weekend. Go train some animals, go twist, go bend. If you surprise us all with success, I’ll make you the most beautiful dress.” In the morning, Roxy sang: “Rise and Shine; today, I’ll find a show of mine.”

She first went to her friend, June, who came from the colorful planet, Balloon. Roxy told her she was in a fix and that she counted on her bag of tricks. With her round face so cute and so smiley, she did the headline show: “The Funny Clown June O’Reily.” She literally had a bag of tricks; from which she’d pull a bunch of rainbow-colored sticks. Then, she’d utter the magic word “Jelle-Jollie-Beanie-Bollie”. The sticks would move, dance and swirl as her chubby fingers would twirl. Fireworks would come out of the sticks as with her hands they were flicked.

Like her friend June, Roxy would put on the make-up, blow her cheeks and bear up. She dipped her hand in the bag of tricks to find that there are no sticks. From the balloon tent, Roxy ran out. She was so annoyed and wanted to shout. Yet, Roxy was very strong. She knew that letting go of her dream was wrong. Roxy said: “It’s Okay. I’m fine… I’ll find another show of mine.”

The next tent was so huge in size that she couldn’t see the flag up in the skies. She had a friend living in there called Majestic; he was a foreigner from the planet Elastic. They had the most remarkable agility and that was among their versatility. They can swing from one end of the globe until the other end with a rope.

Roxy felt she had a chance and on the top of the ladder she’d prance. She swallowed her slaver and cleared her throat; she was ready or so she thought. She put some powder on her square fingertips; she grabbed the swing and bit her lips. Above the din, she’d hear her heart pounding vigorously even before she’d start.

She closed her eyes and off she swung; she drew some air into her lung. Quickly, she felt her grip loosening and her soul sank as she landed on a net and her eyes went blank. She jumped off the net and started to flee when Majestic said: “You’ll find another thing; C’est La Vie.” Roxy said: “It’s Okay. I’m fine… I’ll find another show of mine.”

She went and woke her best friend, Fluffy. Oh My! His eyes were yet so puffy. In a house so fuzzy like a teddy bear’s, fluffy brushed his teeth and combed his hair. He took her hands and they walked straight to the dogs’ house. She didn’t feel great. Fluffy wasn’t only the best dog trainer, he was holding the title “The Best Entertainer”.

The dogs were all so cute but she saw them as hostile. Hence, Roxy stood in a faraway isle. Finally, when fluffy told her to come in; the butterflies in her stomach started to win. She bit her square tongue, for that is what they say would keep the stray dogs away. She stretched her arms to a dog of cute charms. Yet, when she threw a ball at him he only wagged his tail and licked her chin. She knew it just then; she didn’t have a pet-trainer within. Roxy said: “It’s Okay. I’m fine… I’ll find another show of mine.”

She left and sat alone, so blue. What could her show be?! She had no clue. She had to start the most important job, to go and fumble in her wardrobe. Towards home, she started to sprint; maybe she’ll find something that will give her a hint. Mrs. Boxy came in with a box in her hand; it had pink bow on it so grand. She quickly undid the bow, to find a pointe as white as snow.

All of a sudden, she had a plan that would make her have a million fans. She decided to make her own original dance and with training she will enhance. She waited till the trainings end and sneaked into the main tent. Roxy said: “It was dark in there and that is fine; now, the entire dancing floor is mine”. She stood on her square tiptoes and twirled around without flaws. She continued to twist and glide and while doing this, time flied. She created the most wonderful dance; she didn’t know who was having a glance.

Mr. Hawks have seen the whole show and from behind the curtains he yelled: “Bravo Bravo”. He promised she will do her dance in a fancy act worthy of a chance. She told her mum who made her the dress. “You look beautiful” Mr. Boxy did confess. All the family gathered in there. Yes, in the main tent, there was excitement in the air.

The circus then announced: “Prepare yourself for the headline show: The most wonderful dance in the Shadow.” Out of nowhere a white curtain was drawn all around the ring. It was all dark except for a spotlight on a tiny little thing. They all wondered: “Who did this beautiful prance in such a blur?” as all the audience could see was a shadow of her.

Finally, as the tiny dancer bowed, all the audience stood and did applaud. They were all baffled: “From which planet was she?” They never thought it would be from the lovely family of Mr. Boxy. Then a silky pink dress came to sight and a smile shone in the place so bright. She stood one last time on her tiptoes; then everyone would throw a rose. Roxy stretched her arms and took a flower to remind her of this memorable hour.
