The Cinematic Orchestra A drone that follows you and captures your motion on an animated character

  • Created by: Radwa Hamed, Mennatullah Yahia, Islam Diaa
  • Completed in: Spring 2015
  • Skills: Robotics / Computer Graphics / Automatic Control

    Badges earned on this project:

  • Robotics

  • Synchronization

Project Idea:

The Cinematic Orchestra is our final project for the embedded systems course. This course was a competition based course where final project were presented to a panel of professors, industrial gurus from Valeo, Microsoft Research Labs and Amazon. The project won First Place. The project is composed of two-folds. First, a drone is set to autonomously fly following a specific color of interest, as shown in the video. The irobot Roomba then follows this drone through a synchronization communication module between them. The Roomba carries a Kinect Camera that maps the movement of the person followed. It then connects to an animation that is projected through a portable projector. This animated character mimics the movement of the person being followed as shown in the second half of the video.

The purpose of this project was to create a non invasive motion sensing module that enables Computer Generated Imagery on the move.The idea that a character on the move can be candidly followed with cameras that capture all his movements without having to wear hundreds of motion sensors. These movements are then mapped to the CGI being generated.
