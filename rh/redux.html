Project Info:

With a chance to redo the identity project, I decided to convey the growth that happened during the the quarter. This interactive set explores the meaning of how language can be used to show one's identity and how the artists morphs her multitude of identites in every day life.

The idea is each one in my cohort presses a button that says this is a safe place and then my identity shines out.The first statement meant "The light you shine on others reflects on you". This one used light diffusing acrylic. The second one meant "Stand at the corner of your dream and fight" using the red acrylic. The third meant "I am free" in green. The fourth meant "My hand doesn't bend, my heart doesn't falter and I don't fall" in blue.

My Role:

Individual Project

I decided to own my identity and use it to shine how strong of a persson I am after sharing so much vulnerability on the first iteration of the project, showing my identity under the microscope. The piece was created using colored acrylic, light diffusing acrylic, foam core and light bulbs.

I worked on most of the calligraphic pieces, excpet two pieces which were an adaptaton of the work of other artists. I also created the installation itself. The array of lights was powered using AC Voltage. Essam Alnaggar, another engineer helped created the circuit and validate its safety.
