Project Idea:

The MENA Eco System Gallery is an interactive Gallery where visitors step on a pressure sensor embedded on a step to automatically start a video that provides them with data on the entrepreneurial ecosystem in the corresponding country.

Rise Up Summit is the biggest Entrepreneurial Summit in the MENA region. It has been held on annual basis in the heart of Cairo since 2013.

The gallery was deisgned to be a silent space for visiotrs to contemplate the growth of the market and invesmtents in the region within the last 5 years- post Arab Spring. Their were multiple essential elements in the design including: the data, the data representation, the interactive design, the aesthetic layout. The data collected was designed in a way to tell the story of the state being studied. A video of the data of each state was projected directly from the ceiling. 10 states were selcted in this gallery namely: Egypt, UAE, Palestine, Saudi, Lebanon, Kuwait, Bahrain, Jordan, Tunisia and Morroco.
