Project Idea:

The MENA Eco System Gallery is an interactive Gallery where visitors step on a pressure sensor embedded on a step to automatically start a video that provides them with data on the entrepreneurial ecosystem in the corresponding country.

It was deisgned to be a silent space for visiotrs to contemplate the growth of the market and invesmtents in the region within the last 5 years- post Arab Spring. Their were multiple essential elements in the design including: the data, the data representation, the interactive design, the aesthetic layout. The data collected was designed in a way to tell the story of the state being studied. The data was presented in the format of a video in the RU color palette. It was designed to be projected directly from the ceiling as a light beam in the middle of dark areas in between - highlighting the turmoil in other areas in the region. 10 states were selcted in this gallery namely: Egypt, UAE, Palestine, Saudi, Lebanon, Kuwait, Bahrain, Jordan, Tunisia and Morroco.

Rise Up Summit is the biggest Entrepreneurial Summit in the MENA region. It has been held on annual basis in the heart of Cairo since 2013.

The Gallery also hosted a networking event were lead investors and entrepreneurs represnting every country hosted in the gallery were present for one-on-one discussion of the presented data

I was leading this Gallery alongside Mahmoud Elheiny with a team of volunteers, graphic designers, spatial designers, engineers and data researchers.

I believe in the power of Impact, Empathy & Innovation.
