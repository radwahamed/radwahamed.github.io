The idea of this project was inspired by the fact that when I first came to the US I felt like my identity was under the microscope and always under scrutiny. It is both intimidating and painful. This was my way of sharing this feeling with others.

My Role:

Individual Project

This interactive set explores the meaning of buildable identity and how the artists morphs her multitude of identites in every day life.

Based on 12 pieces in the cantour museum, I created 12 charcoal drawings of different pieces of my identity, then redrew those art pieces at 0.2mm scale.

I then told the viewers to use a handmade microscope and their phones to see the magnified image and check its color. Based on this image and color, there was a look up table that tells them which part of my identity are they sseeing right now and what does it mean
