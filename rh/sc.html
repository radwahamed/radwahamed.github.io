Project Info:

After working on the dessign bootcamp and delivering the build session about prototyping in a team of 3, one of the attendees approached me to help recreate the bootcamp in an elementary school in Santa Cruz. The opportunity to work with school board memebers, adminstration, parents and kids was both exciting and exhilirating. I worked with a team from the school to prepare and deliver the worksshop.

Alongside the teacher running the makers' lab, we delivered a three hour workshop.

My Role:

Instructor

This was sone of the most generative workshops that I ran. The kids and the parents both learnt to build on each others' ideas, to drop the fear of being judged and to be as creative as possible

As you can see in the photos, they tried solving problems that they faced while travelling. One problem that kept coming up was the problem of car sickness. You can see kids working with other kids on developing these solutions including wearables, or even sight barriers for kids to look in a straight direction.It was inspiring to see how everyone was on-their-feet with prototyping, asking questions and getting feedback.
