UN World Tourism Organization Startup Competition

3rd Place Winner

As part of Egypt's 2030 vision, working on enhancing touristic experience is of immense importance. With a team of two other engineers, we worked on creating a fully immersive tour guide experience, relying on low-energy bluetooth beacons. The idea was to turn Egypt into an open museum; simply by using a bluetooth signal, the person with the app can hear the story of the monument or district or even restaurant they are passing by and be fully immersed in its history without ever having to get detached from the real world and take out their phone. We won third place and were awarded by the minster of tourism.
